  • CPU Usage: 75%
  • Priority: Above Normal

TIER 3: ENTRY-LEVEL CPUs (Intel i3, Ryzen 3, 4-5 threads)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Examples: i3-12100, i3-10100, Ryzen 3 3200G
Settings:
  • Threads: 60% (conservative)
  • CPU Usage: 60%
  • Priority: Normal
Result: Good performance, no lag

TIER 4: LOW-END CPUs (Dual-core, old CPUs, <4 threads)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Examples: Pentium, Celeron, old Core 2 Duo
Settings:
  • Threads: 50% (very conservative)
  • CPU Usage: 50%
  • Priority: Normal
Result: PC won't lag, but slower mining

SPEED-BASED:
├─ Slow CPU (<2 GHz): Reduces load by 10%
